Hanif Fermanda Putra

A highly motivated researcher and developer with PhD in Computer Engineering. Experienced in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), specializing in interaction devices and XR (Extended Reality) technologies. Possesses a strong background in Research and Development, contributing to innovative projects and pushing the boundaries of interactive experiences. Expertise includes interaction devices including IoT, user interface design, simulation, and machine learning. Adept at prototyping and data analysis, passionate about creating interactive technologies. I'm now a research fellow in Saitama Prefecture, Japan.
